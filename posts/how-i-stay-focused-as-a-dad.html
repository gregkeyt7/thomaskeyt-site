How I Stay Focused As a Dad

Published: March 25, 2025

As a father to two beautiful girls, every day is a gift and a challenge. Staying focused in the midst of family, business, and spiritual growth is not easy—but it's my mission.

I start my mornings with quiet time—tea, scripture, prayer. Then comes my health. A strong body supports a strong mind. After that, I carve out time to build: Arion, SnapFlow, my personal development, my finances.

But above all else, I remember why I'm doing this—Taylor, Rayven, and Rachel. They are my why. They center me. Every decision filters through this: will it make our lives richer, stronger, more full of love and purpose?

If you're a parent, stay locked in on your mission. Your kids are watching how you rise.
