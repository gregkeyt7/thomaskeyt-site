Faith Over Fear: My Journey With Christ

Published: March 10, 2025

The world is loud. The news is scary. The pressures of life are real. But in the center of it all, I’ve found my peace—faith in Jesus Christ.

My walk with Christ is not something I do on Sundays. It's in every decision, every plan, every step forward. I fast not just for health—but for clarity. I tithe not just for giving—but for obedience.

Building wealth without spiritual direction is like building a skyscraper on sand. I need my foundation solid. I want Arion and all my businesses to reflect the wisdom and purpose that comes from above.

I choose faith over fear. Because fear limits. But faith? Faith unleashes destiny.
